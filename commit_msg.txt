Initial commit: TD_NLE TouchDesigner-native modular NLE

Fixed-shell player architecture (scene3d/scene2d/fx/video/audio), DAT-table
EDL, 6 Python extensions (extTransport, extSequencer, extPlayerPool,
extAnalysis, extRecorder, extIngest), drag-and-drop media ingest, nondestructive

editor UI layer (viewport, ruler, selection, transport bar).
